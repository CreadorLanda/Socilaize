# Socialize Product Context

## Summary
Socialize is an open-source, privacy-focused messaging platform with modern UI and deep customization. It supports direct chat, groups, channels, communities, and media sharing with a strong emphasis on trust and user control.

## Users
- People who want a secure, customizable messenger.
- Community owners and creators publishing updates via channels.
- Users who care about privacy, clarity, and speed in everyday messaging.

## Jobs to be done
- Send and receive messages quickly and reliably.
- Follow channels and stay updated without noise.
- Control privacy, identity, and notifications.

## Tone and voice
Clean, calm, trustworthy, and lightly playful. Familiar patterns first, polish second.

## Principles
- Privacy and user control first.
- Customization without clutter.
- Familiar messaging patterns with modern refinement.
- Clear states and predictable behavior.

## Non-goals
- No dark patterns, spyware, or hidden tracking.
- Not a clone of any single messenger.

## References
- WhatsApp for familiar interaction patterns.
- Modern tools for clarity, speed, and polish.
